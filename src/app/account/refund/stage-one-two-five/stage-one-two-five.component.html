Request Options

By clicking on the submit , the request will be forwarded to the Office of the Bursary for Resolution
Note: Application can be set to only Fulfill or Reject at this stage.

By clicking on the "Submit" button , the request will be forwarded to the Student to write an application letter on the subject matter "Refund of Fees" for resolution.
Note: Application can be set to only Fulfill or Reject at this stage.

By clicking on the "Submit" button , You are agreeing to sign and endorse the student application letter the request will be forwarded to the destinated office for resolution on the subject matter "Refund of Fees".

This is the last stage of the application. You can only fulfill or reject the application which would mean the Student would or not get a refund.
